About Vijayam College

Vijayam Scicence and Arts Degree College is affiliated to S.V. University Tirupati. The College is situated in the heart of the city. This is very helpful for the students commuting from far off places in the city, especially for girl students. Vijayam Science and Arts Degree College was conceived to impart quality education in the society. We are proud to say that the college is unique in imparting value based education and our students are well placed in organizations of repute both. The Institution aims to guide the youth towards an enriching creativity and love towards learning, the right attitude, interests in sports, cultural activities and essential skills.

Best degree college

VSADC is one among the best degree College in the chittoor, and is also the most preferred institution for aspiring students and their parents. VSADC has earned itself the reputation of being a centre of academic excellence in many ways over the years.

Life skills
